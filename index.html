About the Fest

MudraNite 2025@TIET is more than just an event – it is a celebration of creativity, knowledge, and togetherness. Over the course of three vibrant days, students from across departments and colleges will come together to showcase their talents, compete in diverse events, and create lasting memories. The fest will feature a mix of cultural, technical, and fun activities ranging from energetic dance battles, thought-provoking debates, and thrilling hackathons to creative photography contests and electrifying musical performances. It is a platform where innovation meets passion, and where students not only compete but also collaborate, learn, and grow.
