About the Events

Our college fest brings together a vibrant mix of cultural, sports, and artistic events, giving students a platform to showcase their talent, creativity, and team spirit. From soulful singing to electrifying dance performances, thrilling sports battles to mesmerizing art displays—there’s something for everyone. Join us in celebrating talent, passion, and togetherness as we create unforgettable memories through these exciting competitions! The events are designed not just for competition, but for fun, learning, and building friendships. Whether you love the stage, the sports ground, or the art corner, there’s an event waiting for you.
